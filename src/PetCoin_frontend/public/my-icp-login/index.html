Donation Recipient Workflow

Follow these simple steps to manage your donations

1. Login with Internet Identity

The recipient logs in to the platform using their Internet Identity to obtain a unique Principal ID.

2. Click the Donate button

Navigate to the donation section and press the donate button.

3. Connect Plug Wallet

Link your Plug Wallet to the platform for secure blockchain transactions.

4. Top up for donation

Add ICP or CPHW tokens to your wallet to fund your donation.

5. Collect all animal cards

Purchase and collect all animal cards as a token of your donation.
